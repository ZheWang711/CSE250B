# Multivariate Gaussian

## Univariate Gaussian
$$var(x) = \Bbb{E}(X - \mu)^2 = \Bbb{E}(X^2) - \mu^2,  where \mu = \Bbb{E}(X)$$

$$std(X) = \sqrt{var(X)}$$

The Univariate Gaussian

$$N(\mu, \sigma^2)$$
$$p(x) = \frac{1} {(2\pi\sigma^2)^{1/2}} exp(- \frac{(x-\mu)^2} {2\sigma^2})$$
